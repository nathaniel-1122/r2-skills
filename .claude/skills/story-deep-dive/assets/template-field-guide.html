How to use this guide

The system. Each chapter opens with THE GIST (one line) and a What you'll learn box, and closes with Takeaways and Questions to ask. Colored badges sort the boxes at a glance: § terms, ! gotcha, ✓ takeaways, ? questions. Every claim carries a numbered, clickable source.
